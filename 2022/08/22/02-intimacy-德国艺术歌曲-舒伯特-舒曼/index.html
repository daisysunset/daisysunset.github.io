Themes in Romantic Poetry and the lied

  • Subjectivity - Emphasis on interiority and inwardness(Wordsworth’s “Inner eye”)
  • Poet as outcast - Romantic artists often present themselves through fictional personae as outcasts, marginal figures isolated from the everyday exchanges of commerce and conventional behavior 痛苦、边缘
  • Nature as mirror - Nature is often presented as a sympathetic being, mirroring or resonating with the speaker’s subjectivity
  • Memory - Present experience is mediated through the past
  • Death - Death is often presented as the culmination 终点 of the desire or longing for what cannot be possessed (ideal love)
